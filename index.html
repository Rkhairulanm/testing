About Us

PT Hasanudin is a reliable B2B partner providing high-quality household essentials across three main categories: stationery, baby supplies, and kitchenware, along with other necessities. We focus on customer satisfaction by offering top-notch products and exceptional service, ensuring your business always has the best for success.

VISION

To become a trusted agent that provides a variety of high-quality stationery, baby supplies, and kitchenware, as well as being the top choice for customers across Indonesia.

MISSION

  • To provide comprehensive, high-quality, and affordable essential products.
  • To provide friendly, fast, and professional service to customers.
  • To continuously innovate in order to meet customer needs and expectations.
  • To build long-term relationships with business partners and customers.

Our Product

Stationery

We offer a wide range of stationery designed to meet your daily needs, whether at home, school, or the office. Our stationery is available in various colors, designs, and sizes, allowing you to tailor your selection to your preferences and requirements.

Baby Supplies

We offer a wide range of baby supplies designed to provide comfort and safety for your little one. Each product is carefully selected to ensure the materials used are gentle on your baby's skin, safe, and free from harmful chemicals.

Kitchenware

We offer a variety of kitchenware designed to make cooking and preparing daily meals easier for you. Each kitchen tool is made from high-quality materials, such as stainless steel, ceramic, and non-stick coatings, ensuring durability, ease of use, and perfect cooking results.
